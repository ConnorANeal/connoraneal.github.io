D&D Resources

D&D Beyond

D&D Beyond is your one-stop shop for just about everything Dungeons & Dragons. On their website, you can purchase all of the D&D Rules and Sourcebooks, as well as different campaign setting guides. Once you own copies of those books, you are also able to look up any information about D&D on their website as if it's a wiki full of the information from books you've purchased. They also have their own character creator.

D&D Beyond

Roll20

Roll20 is a similarly useful website for Dungeons & Dragons players. They have a full wiki on just about everything you need to know about D&D. This website also caters towards players who play online with friends far away. They have a virtual tabletop simulator that allows DMs to show their players maps with tokens for each player and any creatures on the battlefield.

Roll20

D&D Entertainment

Critical Role

Critical Role is a popular Dungeons & Dragons show that started in 2015 on Twitch. Now on their 3rd campaign, there is no shortage of long-form D&D content available on their YouTube and Twitch channels, and their own streaming service, Beacon. Their campaign content has captured the hearts of fans for years, even starting a Prime Video original animated series called The Legend of Vox Machina based on the story of their first D&D campaign.

Critical Role on YouTube Critical Role on Twitch Beacon

Dimension 20

Dimension 20 is another popular Dungeons & Dragons show that started in 2018 on Dropout. Dimension 20 has run several campaigns over the last several years, as well as smaller side quest campaigns where they rotated Dungeon Masters each time.

Character Creation

This page will include a lot of the information you will need to know before you go about creating your first character for Dungeons & Dragons 5th Edition, as well as some general tips that will be helpful when it comes to creating a character backstory for an TTRPG setting.

1. General Character Idea

First thing you should do before diving into the mechanics of any TTRPG, is to decide what kind of a character you want to play. This will vary drastically from player to player, but this often involves coming up with the beginnings of a backstory that sounds interesting to you. It's important not to get too bogged down in all the details of the story in the early stages, but deciding the kind of story you want to tell might inform the decisions you make once you dive into the mechanical part of character creation.

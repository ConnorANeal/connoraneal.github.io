Character Class

First, you can pick your class. Your class determines what kind of firepower and utility you bring to combat. Classes generally fall into three categories: Frontline, Half Caster, and Full Caster.
Frontline classes specialize in fighting enemies up close relying on their strength and weapons to deal damage. Full Casters generally have less hit points, but deal a lot of damage or produce useful effects using spells. Half Casters take some of the utility of spellcasting and use it to power up their weapon attacks.

Character Stats

Finally, you can insert your stats here. In case you do not know what your stats should be, here's how to determine your stats.
Start by rolling four d6 dice. Remove the die that rolled the lowest. Add together the three remaining dice, and that is one of your stats. You get to pick which attribute that stat is applied to. Repeat this method for all six stat attributes. No stat should end up being lower than 3 or higher than 18 after adding up the dice.

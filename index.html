About Dungeons & Dragons

Dungeons & Dragons is a popular Tabletop Roleplaying Game (TTRPG) created by Wizards of the Coast in 1974. The point of D&D is to tell a story with the aid of your Dungeon Master. This story is told through a hours of dialogue, combat, and dice rolling.
